From architectural curtain walls to precision decorative glass — Jazan Group delivers 48 glass color variants, computer-driven CNC fabrication, and ISO-certified quality to clients across the region.

48-Color Glass Catalog

Our signature catalog covers 48 distinct glass colors and finishes — from ultra-clear float and deep bronze tints to vibrant chromatic panels, acid-etched frosted glass, and high-performance solar-reflective coatings.

Every color is stocked in standard thicknesses (4mm–19mm) with same-week CNC cut-to-size availability. Custom tints and screen-printed designs are available on order.

GLASS COLOR CATALOG — 48 VARIANTS

Precision CNC Cutting

Every order processed at Jazan Group passes through our digital fabrication pipeline. Customer drawings are imported as DXF files, nested algorithmically for maximum yield, and executed on 5-axis CNC waterjet and diamond-blade cutting systems.

Our tolerance is ±0.1mm across all thicknesses. Every panel receives a unique QR-linked digital passport documenting the full production record.
